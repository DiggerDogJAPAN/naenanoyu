Naena no Yu

ー　Natural Hotsprings　ー

Suginosawa's only day-trip hot spring facility. The spring is a hydrogen carbonate spring, which is characterized by an almost colorless and transparent water with little peculiarity. Nearby are sightseeing spots such as Sasagamine Kogen, Mt Hiuchi, Naena Falls, and Suginohara Ski Resort. Feel free to use the onsen after mountain climbing, camping, skiing, or just for a day trip to the hot spring.

Naena-no-Yu Facilities

Open Space

There is a large room on the 1st floor and a small room on the 2nd floor, which can be used as a area to rest.

Handicapped Assistance

There is a slope at the entrance of the facility and a chair lift to the second floor for the physically challenged. (The building is not completely barrier-free so caregiver support maybe required.)

Vending Machines

Vending machines selling various drinks and ice cream are available.

Massage Chair

There are two massage chairs on the second floor.
